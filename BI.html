New to Bold BI?

What is Bold BI?

Bold BI is a powerful business intelligence platform that enables users to create, analyze, and share interactive dashboards and reports. It supports both self-service analytics for business users and embedded analytics for developers.

Why use Bold BI?

Bold BI empowers organizations to make data-driven decisions with ease. It offers intuitive tools, seamless integration, and robust security to cater to both non-technical users and developers.

What can you build with Bold BI?
With Bold BI, you can design interactive and highly customizable dashboards tailored to your business needs. Additionally, developers can embed these analytics into applications, like our live Healthcare Appointment Planner, enabling seamless integration of real-time insights into your software solutions.
Do I need technical skills to use Bold BI?

No, Bold BI is designed for users of all skill levels. Business users can easily create and explore analytics with a user-friendly interface, while developers have access to advanced tools for embedding analytics.

Can I share dashboards with my team?

Absolutely! Bold BI allows you to easily share dashboards and reports with your team through secure links, email, or embedded integrations.
